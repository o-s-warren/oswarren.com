Valve Attention


Catastrophic mumbling through heart-rendered

perusing the moment's sunkenness. Meanwhile

Significance — leaning back and forth trodden,

Aspects of an half-remembered archive trawl.

We don’t talk about that except as heavy air or

Nerve-angled chattering. Law of the room as

Drawn off Father into channels or conduits or viaducts

(But most importantly, stoppages). Gesturally,

It’s motions concordant with habit but eased and

Made gradual — ‘It’ being both 1. his new movements

Attuned to extension or lasting, and 2. the pattern

Of a delicate childhood all tip-toes and wading. Thanks

Though for the do-over, knowing lode-bearing

Deficiencies and its actual justness.
