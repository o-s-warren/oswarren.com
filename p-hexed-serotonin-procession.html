Hexed Serotonin Procession


Good of me to thrill this way, eye-rolled, and crumbling.

Uh huh and I’m listening like you say to them, and I’m glancing then

And bitten off, HSP. Overwritten like it’s frail or too heavy, limned


Promising not to enmesh demand with eased One:

What’s lighter than being seen Two: It’s slipped, given

to Three: Fast parching and keeling cruel. Okay but

Down mountains to patios you take care of it, my bleating heart
