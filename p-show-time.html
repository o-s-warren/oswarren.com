Show Time


Fair-weather granular, take tether in a scoring board. Fanged

In tribute, twenty seconds desiccation then leavened

As a crawling pattern. Run rings wrought and sedentary,

Breathe out. Further out we never knew, to us who ought.

Slather myopic encouragement dressed in weighting to the

Time. Cawing like forever. Insistent as a strain under

Medicine’s thin-thawing roughage. Waiting now for

Line and light, marched forward with anodyne honesty.


Sheathed and cloven unlike yes taken but residuary.

Nought but them that claimed fallowed grips in

Hearts grown diminished. Painted nails and granite

Semblances I’ve shown it that I’ve shown it
