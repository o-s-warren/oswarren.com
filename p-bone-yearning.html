Bone-yearning


Glances angular as if from without, creaked in turn and bone-yearning.

To scrawled apologies submit the second stressed debt, to crinkled

Savagery and forthrightness. Steeped within withoutness, testy young

Thing you said with the marrow sapped. Additional oblation under
