Quintessential Ties


Walk back and forever ago there’s something

Worth sorting through, like lip-liner anagrams. I’m

guessing here’s the place for a nested

co-terminal, convertible under the sign.

Don’t grin grin and pirouette if there’s no

Place to pull them from. Over and over. Let’s

still see living in the age, stone dry and cracking.
